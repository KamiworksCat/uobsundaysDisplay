Let your kids learn the science behind their favorite games!

Bring your kids for a 90 minute introduction to basic coding with our UOB Sunday Banking ambassadors, where they will be able to develop their very first game with popular coding programmes like Lightbots, Kodu and Code.org. It's the best way to nurture their computational and problem solving skills!

This program is in partnership with the Infocomm Development Authority of Singapore (IDA), designed for kids from 8 – 12 years old.

Every Sunday at Serangoon Central (Nex) and Jem UOB branches from 14 to 28 June 2015.

Session 1 : 11.30 am – 1.00 pm
Session 2 : 1.30 pm – 3.00 pm

Spaces are limited and are on a first-come, first-served basis, so register early to avoid disappointment. To ensure that other children also get the opportunity to participate in these activities, parents can only register their child once for any of the activities organised.
